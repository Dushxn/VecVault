# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in the VecVault project harassment-free.

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Respectful communication.
- Welcoming environment.
- Accepting constructive criticism.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Disruptive trolling.

## Enforcement
Report violations to: support@vecvault.dev

Project maintainers may take appropriate corrective actions.
